What is Airport Scape?

Airport Scape is the game and application that we have developed for the subject of Disseny de Serveis i Aplicacions (DSA)

In this game you are a "traveller" that will find some obstacles to reach the boarding gate... Try to reach it on time and win the maximum amount of money!

In the webpage you will find the menu to log in, register, info about the stats of the users and the shop, where you will be able to buy your suitcase or bag and objects so as to go unnoticed in the security control... Good luck!
